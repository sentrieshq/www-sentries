PRE-MINT TERMS AND CONDITIONS

TL;DR:
NFTs will be locked upon mint and will unlock over the course of 30 days.

FULL TERMS

  • A “Frozen Token” is defined as a Non-Transferable, Non-Tradable token.

  • A “Thawed Token” is defined as a Transferable, Tradable token.

  • Formula: Number of NFTs minted / 30 days = Number unlocked per day.

Prior to the whitelist minting, Sentries will hold a pre-mint round for eligible individuals (“Capitalist” role in Discord) with the ability to mint a minimum of fifteen (15) and no more than one (100) hundred Sentries NFTs (the "Pre-Mint Round").

Sentries NFTs generated during the Pre-Mint Round shall be Frozen Tokens.

Beginning seventy-two (72) hours post Pre-Mint Round each wallet's allocation will begin to Thaw according to the total quantity of NFTs minted, divided by thirty (30) twenty-four (24) hour intervals (the "Schedule").

For sake of clarity, if a wallet minted ninety (90) Frozen NFTs during the Pre-Mint Round, three (3) NFTs will become Thawed Tokens each twenty-four (24) hour period until the end of the thirty (30) intervals (90/30 = 3). Additionally, Frozen Tokens shall be considered staked and receive any and all value accrued during the Schedule.

By participating in the Pre-Mint Round you agree to have these NFTs frozen and thawed according to the Schedule and are subject to the terms herein.
The authorities on these Frozen tokens, contracts, and interactions are that of the Sentries Administrators and Blocksmith Labs.
